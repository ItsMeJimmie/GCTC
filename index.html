Generic Campus Tour Company

Introduction

Our company is called Generic Campus Tour Company (GCTC), and our goal is to help you navigate the Wright State University Main Dayton campus. Our program aims to use the Godot game engine to build a fully fleshed out 3D map to produce a 3D campus navigation system. Our product will allow you to do three main things which are as follows.

  • Enter in two locations and get the quickest route between them.
  • Upload your semester scehdule and build the quickest route between each class
  • Navigate through different parts of the 3D map to look at important university landmarks.
